Welcome

Young Women's Camp is on Yagaji Island, March 25–28, 2026.

Drop-off: March 25 at 3:00 PM at the Okinawa District Building. Please do not be late!

Pickup: March 28 at noon at the Okinawa District Building.

Meeting Location: Okinawa District Building, 7 Chome-11-1 Matsumoto, Okinawa, 904-2151.

YW Camp Packing List

  • 3 changes of weather-appropriate active clothes
  • At least one pair of long pants for hiking
  • Socks and underwear
  • Pajamas
  • Swimsuit
  • Jacket
  • Raincoat or poncho
  • Personal hygiene items (including shampoo and conditioner)
  • Sunscreen and bug spray
  • Hat
  • Towel
  • Water shoes or felt-bottomed boots
  • Shower shoes
  • Good hiking shoes
  • Comfortable camp shoes
  • Day bag (small backpack)
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Scriptures
  • Flashlight
  • Sleeping bag
  • Sleeping pad
  • Tent (if signed up to bring one)
